About

Bridge Music Academy

Bridge Music Academy was formed in 2009 with a vision to offer International standard education in Western Music in India. Currently over 600 students learn at 5 branches of the Academy. Bridge Music Academy also manages and runs western music programs at high profile institutions such as IIT – Delhi, Salwan Public School, St Joseph School, Hope Academy (Nagaland), Khaitan Public School to name a few.

The first branch of the Academy was setup in New Rajinder Nagar in West Delhi and soon expanded to Dwarka and Chandigarh. Each faculty member is a specialist at their subject and teaches only the discipline they specialize in. More than 90% of students take one-to-one lessons tailored to their needs.

Why Choose Trinity Access?

At Trinity Access, we believe that music is more than a skill — it's an experience that shapes confidence, creativity, and discipline. Whether you're picking up an instrument for the first time or advancing your craft, we guide you with structured lessons, stage experiences, and certified courses designed to meet international standards.
